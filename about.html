🐾 About Pet Nav

Pet Nav is a resource navigation platform focused on the pet niche. We collect, organize, and review the best pet-related websites and communities on the internet, helping pet owners quickly find the information and services they need.

🎯 Our Mission

To help every pet owner easily access scientific and reliable pet care knowledge. Whether you are a new or experienced pet owner, Pet Nav provides curated high-quality resources, saving you time searching and verifying information.

📖 Our Story

Pet Nav was founded by a team of pet lovers. During our daily pet care routines, we found that the internet was filled with mixed-quality information, and high-quality pet resources were scattered across different corners without a systematic curation platform. So we decided to build this navigation site ourselves.

Starting from just a few dozen resource links, we continuously collected, curated, and updated, gradually building a resource library covering eight major categories: Dogs, Cats, Fish, Small Pets, Health, Food, Shopping, and Community. Every resource included has been manually reviewed and tested.
